Our Heritage

Founded on the belief that a fragrance is a personal signature, AURA is a contemporary fragrance house rooted in classic French perfumery. We synthesize natural, sustainably sourced oils and resins with modern molecular science to formulate scent portfolios that are rich, long-lasting, and intensely personal.

We reject mass-production. We do not design for the shelves; we design for the skin. Each formulation is aged, blended, and bottled under strict supervision in our lab, maintaining a direct line to the traditions of the trade.

"Perfume is the most intense form of memory. It is a silent signature, an aura that remains in a room long after you have departed."
— Master Perfumer, AURA
